Quick Tacos

Delicious and fast protein to last

Enjoy the flavors of Mexico with these easy-to-make ground turkey tacos. Packed with lean protein and customizable toppings, these tacos are a delicious and healthy option for any weeknight meal. The savory ground turkey is seasoned to perfection and served in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings, such as salsa, avocado, and cilantro.

    Ingredients

  • 1lb of 99% lean ground turkey (Or ground meat/alternative of your choice)
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 can of roasted corn
  • 1 stalk of green onion
  • 1 small white onion
  • 1 Package of tortillas or tortillas chips
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    Steps

  1. Prepare your green onion and white onion by chopping them up finely
  2. Pour your olive oil in the pan and turn your stove on medium
  3. Take your ground meat out of the package and place into the pan
  4. Cook your meat until cooked to safety standards
  5. Drain any excess fats and oils from the pan if needed at this point
  6. Push all meat to one side of your pan and place both onion cuts on the bare side
  7. Cook this for about 2 minutes on low heat
  8. When the time is up, add your black beans and roasted corn to the pot and mix it all up
  9. Add a half of a cup of water and place the lid on your pan. The food will now simmer for about 3-5minutes
  10. Your meal is now complete! You can now pull out your bag of chips or warm up a tortilla - your choice!
