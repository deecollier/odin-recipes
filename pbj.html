PB&J

Description

Enjoy a classic childhood favorite with this simple and satisfying peanut butter and jelly sandwich. This timeless combination of creamy peanut butter and sweet jelly is a quick and easy snack or light meal. Customize your sandwich with your favorite bread, peanut butter, and jelly for a personalized taste. Whether you are packing a lunch or enjoying a snack at home, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread of choice
  • Butter(plant or milk based)

Steps

  1. First, you will toast and butter two slices of bread.
  2. Open your peanut butter and use a knife to spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Open your jelly and spread it on the other slice
  4. Press both pieces together and enjoy!
