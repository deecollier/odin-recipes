Cheese Pizza

Description

Indulge in the classic comfort food of a homemade cheese pizza. This simple yet satisfying recipe features a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and a generous layer of melted mozzarella cheese. Customize your pizza with your favorite toppings, or keep it classic for a timeless flavor. Whether you are a seasoned chef or a beginner, this recipe is easy to follow and delivers a delicious homemade pizza every time.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of pizza blend shredded cheese
  • 2 Premade pizza dough circles
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees
  2. Take a tspn of olive oil and rub it in a circle on both pizzas dough
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly across your dough. If you prefer a saucier pizza, use it more liberally
  4. Sprinkle cheese across your pizza
  5. Place your pizza in the over for 12 minutes
  6. ENJOY!
